🍝 1. Lasagna

Description:
A classic Italian layered pasta dish made with sheets of lasagna noodles, a rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese.

Ingredients:

For the Meat Sauce:
  • 500g minced beef or chicken
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 3–4 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 2 cups tomato puree or crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
For Béchamel Sauce:
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk (warm)
  • Salt, pepper, and nutmeg (a pinch)
Other:
  • 9 lasagna sheets (boiled or oven-ready)
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese (grated)
  • ½ cup parmesan cheese (optional)

Steps:

  1. Make the meat sauce: Sauté onion and garlic in oil. Add meat and cook until browned. Add tomatoes and herbs. Simmer 20–30 mins until thick.
  2. Prepare béchamel: Melt butter, add flour and stir for 1 min. Slowly add milk while whisking. Season. Cook until creamy.
  3. Layer: In a baking dish, add meat sauce, noodles, béchamel, and cheese. Repeat layers (usually 3 layers), ending with cheese on top.
  4. Bake: Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). Bake 35–40 mins until golden and bubbly. Rest 10 mins before serving.
